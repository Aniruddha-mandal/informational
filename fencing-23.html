Animal Fencing

Animal fencing is a crucial component of wildlife management and agricultural practices, serving to delineate boundaries while simultaneously protecting crops and livestock from intrusion. These fences come in various forms, ranging from traditional wooden or wire constructions to modern electric or netting designs. Their effectiveness lies in their ability to deter animals from entering or exiting certain areas, thus mitigating conflicts between wildlife and human activities.

Additionally, animal fencing plays a vital role in conservation efforts by preventing habitat fragmentation and safeguarding endangered species from human disturbances.
